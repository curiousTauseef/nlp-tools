Notes 
Individual:
He shows as a tailor on the 1850 school census, Rowan Co., NC.

They had five known children. (See LH, Chap. V, pp 29 and 82.) Records shown here on their children are from the 1860 census andOrgan Church.
The 1870 census shows John P., Martha R., and Luther A. at home. The 1880 census, Locke Twp, shows only Martha R. at home. Not foundin 1900.Organ Church Baptismal Record states: (Born) Jun 28, 1823, Criseln

Alexander (Lentz), parents, Peter Lentz." (Notice the garble
"Criseln" for "Wilson.")

Marriage:
Bonded by Henry Klutz, witnessed by John H. Hardie, Jr.. See "Rowan

Marriages 1753-1868," p. 238. In his diary, Rev. Rothrock states: "Tues, Oct 27, 1846--Went to
Widow Eddleman's and married Wilson A. Lentz to Polly Eddleman."
"Rowan Marriages 1753-1868" states: Lentz, Wilson & MaryEddleman, 26 Oct 1846; Henry Kluts, bondsman; John H. Hardie, Jr.,wit."
