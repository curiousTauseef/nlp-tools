Lentz to represent US in competition

Seminole native Amanda Lentz will represent the United States Power Tumbling Team in international competition today through Saturday.

The squad will compete in Paris and Warsaw, Poland.

Lentz, who trains and coaches at Talent Plus in Lubbock, earned her spot on the U.S. team with a strong finish at the national competition earlier this year in Phoenix. She later finished fourth individually at the World Power Tumbling Championships in Vancouver, B.C.
